A directive for scrolling large sets of data.

Below you see you a list of {{total}} elements. Each time when the cached display set is out of reach a call will be made retrieve more data. (Currently we simulate a 2 second timeout and return a set).

Note that infinite is not really infinite, you can really only display around 2147483645 / height of your template. So in this sample we could go upto 700k rows, after that things will just not work.
